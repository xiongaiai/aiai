About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fend off waves of increasingly difficult monsters. As the last human standing, your mission is to survive as long as possible while upgrading your character and unlocking powerful new abilities.

With its addictive gameplay and roguelike elements, Monster Survivors offers a unique experience every time you play. Can you become the ultimate survivor and set a new high score?

Game Details

  • Genre: Survival, Action, Roguelike
  • Platform: Browser (PC & Mobile)
  • Controls: WASD/Arrow Keys to move, Mouse to aim
  • Difficulty: Easy to learn, challenging to master
  • Play Time: 10-30 minutes per run

Survival Tips

Keep Moving

Standing still is a death sentence. Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded by monsters.

Prioritize Upgrades

Choose your upgrades wisely. Focus on damage and area-of-effect abilities early on to clear waves more efficiently.

Know Your Enemies

Different monsters have different behaviors. Learn their patterns to better anticipate their movements.

Collect Experience

Don't forget to collect experience gems dropped by defeated monsters to level up faster.

Use the Environment

Use obstacles and terrain features to your advantage to funnel monsters and create chokepoints.
